Study in Japan

Japan

Japan offers Bangladeshi students the opportunity of studying in a safe environment in one of the most developed countries in the world with 30-100% scholarships and good employability prospects to migrate after graduation.

Why Study in Japan?

World Class Degrees

International students in Japan will receive the benefits of some of the highest educational standards in the world. The Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development, has ranked Japanese high school students number one in the world for mathematics, and number two for scientific literacy.

Scholarships

japanese universities offer scholarships based on Merit, English Proficiency, ECA, and a well-written Essay which can range from 30-100%.

Employment Opportunities

Most students receive job offers even before they graduate. Starting salaries in Japan are one of the highest in the world and the employability rate for students are 90-98% after their graduation.

Immigration in Japan

Low-skilled workers will be able to reside in Japan for five years, while foreign workers with specialized skills will be allowed to stay indefinitely, along with their family members. Japan has one of the best passports in the world, 191 visa-free travels, it’s the strongest passport in the world.

Living Expenses

Living expenses can be 1/4th of what you would pay in countries like the USA or Canada. The average expenses for living in Tokyo are around 100,000 YEN. Living expense stipends are also offered through Jasso scholarships.

Great Reputation

Japan has the highest number of Nobel prize winners of any other Asian country. Japan is one of the safest countries in the world and is reputed to have a culture that is warm, welcoming, and helpful.

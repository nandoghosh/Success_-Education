Beginner Level (N5)

Our Japanese Language Course starts with the basics, focusing on the N5 level of proficiency. This level is designed for students who have yet to gain prior knowledge of the Japanese language. Our N5 course includes the following:

  • The fundamentals of the Japanese language,
  • Including basic vocabulary,
  • Grammar structures, and
  • Essential expressions.
  • Through interactive lessons and practical exercises,
  • Students develop the skills to read, write, listen, and speak at an introductory level.

Intermediate Level (N4)

In addition to our beginner-level (N5) course, we offer an intermediate-level course focusing on N4 proficiency. This level is designed for students who have some prior knowledge of the Japanese language and want to enhance their language skills and understanding further. Our N4 course includes the following:

  • It covers more complex grammar structures,
  • Expands vocabulary,
  • Enhances reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills to achieve a higher level of proficiency.

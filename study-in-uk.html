Study in UK

United Kingdom

The UK is recognized for its status in education with Top-ranked Universities, Academic excellence, a World-class environment for international students, and global career opportunities after completing study in UK.

Why Study in UK

International Reputation

If you’re a graduate of a UK University, you’re part of an elite group of alumni. British universities and their qualifications are recognized internationally for their excellence. The high quality of research and teaching at British universities is acknowledged throughout the world and that means a boost in your career.

Cost-Effective

The UK is considerably less expensive than other destinations that are English-speaking like Australia and the US in the case of the cost of higher education. The UK offers 3-year Under-Grad and 1-year Post-Grad degrees allowing for faster progression to work, business, and social life & you save up on a year’s expenses.

Free Healthcare and Work Opportunity

The United Kingdom has the National Health Service (NHS) which means students studying within the UK for at least six months, have access to healthcare at no added cost. You are able to work as much as 20 hours part-time per week at employment.

Cultural Diversity

The UK is a place of contrasts and culture and diversity is its greatest strength. The cultural diversity of life in British higher education is unrivaled. UK students are increasing every year at every level of education. This means that you’ll be in a community of like-minded people from a variety of backgrounds.

Student Satisfaction

The UK boasts one of the highest student satisfaction rates amongst English speaking countries, with over 90% of the whopping 2.32 million students in the UK happy with their experience. London beat international cities Tokyo (2nd) and Melbourne (3rd) to first place for the second consecutive year making it the best student city.

Post Study Work Visa

Students who have a valid student visa at the time of application and have successfully completed a degree at undergraduate level or above at a Higher Education Provider with a track record of compliance will receive a work visa upto 2-3 years after completion.
